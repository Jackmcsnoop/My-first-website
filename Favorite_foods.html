My favourite foods

Shortbread

Ingredients

  • 125g of butter
  • 55g of caster sugar
    • Plus extra for a finish
  • 180g of plain flower

Method

  1. preheat your oven to 200oC
  2. Mix the butter and suggar with your hands until smooth
  3. Stir in the plain flower to acheive a smooth paste
    • Chill in the fridge for half an hour
  4. Using a knife create shortbread sized pieces of dough sprincling it with caster suggar
  5. Bake in the oven for 15-20 minuites untill golden brown
